Hi, I'm Nate.

I am a data analyst with a broad set of skills to help stakeholders make better decisions.

I primarily use python because of the wide range of libraries available to do everything from data acquisition, processing and statistical analysis, all the way through to complex machine learning modeling and creating sophisticated visualizations. I also enjoy using SQL, Microsoft Excel, Tableau, Command Line or even learning a new tool or technology that can help improve my work.

I was born and raised in the Seattle area, but am now calling Austin, Texas my home. I live on the Eastside with my wife and our two dogs. I am an outdoors enthusiast and love hiking, snowboarding, biking, or even just going for a run around the neighborhood. I have a blue belt in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu and am currently training here. When I’m not coding, or problem solving, you’ll find me in the kitchen, cooking and or eating but probably more of the latter.
